Samosa

  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description : Samosas are crispy pastries filled with a savory mixture of spiced potatoes and peas. These popular snacks are usually served with chutney and are perfect for tea time or as appetizers.

Ingredients

Ingrideints Quantity
Potatoes 3 (boiled and smashed)
Spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala) 1 tbsp each
Green Peas Â½ cup (boiled)
Flour 2 tbsp (for dough)
oil for frying
Salt as per taste
Preparation Time: 30 minnutes

Steps :-

  1. In a bowl, mix boiled and mashed potatoes with boiled peas, spices, and salt.
  2. Combine flour, salt, and enough water to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Divide dough into small balls. Roll out each ball into a circle, cut in half, and form a cone. Fill with the potato mixture and seal the edges.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry samosas on medium heat until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce.
