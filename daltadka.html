Dal Tadka

  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description : Dal tadka is a comforting dish of lentils cooked until creamy, then tempered with spices and aromatics in ghee. It's typically served with rice or roti for a wholesome meal.

Ingredients

Ingrideints Quantity
Toor dal (pigeon peas) 1 cup
onion 1 (finely chopped)
tomatoes 1 (chopped)
turmeric powder ½ tbsp
Cumin seeds 1 tbsp
ginger-garlic paste 1 tbsp
ghee/oil 2 tbsp
Coriander leaves for garnish
Salt as per taste
Preparation Time: 30 minnutes

Steps :-

  1. Rinse toor dal and cook in a pressure cooker with water until soft (about 3-4 whistles). Mash slightly and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee/oil. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomato, turmeric, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  4. Add the cooked lentils to the pan. Mix well and adjust water for desired consistency. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve with rice or roti.
