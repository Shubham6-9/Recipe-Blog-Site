Biryani

  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description : A rich and aromatic rice dish, biryani combines fragrant basmati rice with marinated meat or vegetables, layered with spices and herbs. It’s often garnished with fried onions, coriander, and served with raita.

Ingredients

Ingrideints Quantity
Basmati rice 2 cups
Basmati rice 2 cups
onions 2 (sliced)
tomatoes 2 (chopped)
yogurt 1 cup
ginger-garlic paste 2 tbsp
biryani masala 2 tbsp
Whole spices (cloves, cardamom, bay leaf) 4-5 each
oil/ghee 4 tbsp
Fresh coriander and mint leaves ½ cup (chopped)
Salt as per taste
Preparation Time: 1 hour

Steps :-

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken/mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  2. Rinse basmati rice until water runs clear. Soak for 30 minutes, then boil in salted water until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pot, heat oil/ghee and fry sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  4. In the same pot, add marinated meat and cook until browned. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Layer half of the partially cooked rice over the meat. Sprinkle with whole spices, chopped coriander, and mint leaves. Add the remaining rice on top.
  6. Cover the pot with a tight lid, seal with dough if possible, and cook on low heat for 25-30 minutes until fully cooked.
  7. Gently fluff the biryani and serve with raita or salad.
