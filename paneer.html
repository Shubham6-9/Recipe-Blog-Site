Paneer Tikka

  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description : Paneer tikka features marinated cubes of paneer and colorful bell peppers, grilled to perfection. The smoky flavor and spice make it a popular appetizer or snack.

Ingredients

Ingrideints Quantity
Paneer 250 gm (cube)
Bell peppers 1 (cubed)
onions 1 (cubed)
yougurt ½ cup
tikka masala 2 tbsp
ginger-garlic paste 1 tbsp
lemon juice 1 tbsp
oil 1 tbsp
Salt as per taste
Preparation Time: 30 minnutes

Steps :-

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, tikka masala, lemon juice, oil, and salt. Add paneer cubes, bell peppers, and onion. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the grill or oven to a high temperature (around 200°C/400°F).
  3. Thread marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers.
  4. Place skewers on the grill and cook for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until slightly charred.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
