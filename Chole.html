Chole Bhature

  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description : Chole bhature is a classic North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas served with deep-fried, fluffy bread (bhature). It's a hearty and satisfying meal often enjoyed for breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

Ingrideints Quantity
Chickpeas 1 cup (soaked overnight)
onion 1 (finely chopped)
tomatoes 1 (chopped)
Chole masala 2 tbsp
Flour 2 cups (for bhature)
ginger-garlic paste 1 tbsp
oil 2 tbsp
yougurt ½ cup
Baking powder 1 tbsp
Salt as per taste
Preparation Time: 1 hour

Steps :-

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight. Drain and pressure cook with water and salt until soft (about 4-5 whistles).
  2. In a pan, heat oil and sauté chopped onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Stir in pureed tomato and cook until oil separates. Add cooked chickpeas and chole masala. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix flour, yogurt, baking powder, and salt. Knead into a soft dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. oll out the dough into small circles and deep fry in hot oil until puffed and golden.
  6. Serve hot chole with bhature, garnished with onions and coriander.
